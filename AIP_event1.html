A Connection Through Food: Luna’s LUNpias Ribbon Cutting Ceremony in Poway California

October 28, 2023

On Saturday, October 28, 2023, Luna’s LUNpias , a Filipino style restaurant with a twist, held their ribbon cutting ceremony to celebrate their official opening in Poway, California.

This family owned kitchen and dining was created by Luna, a mother of five, who grew up in the heart of a bustling Filipino kitchen, learning the secrets behind her parent’s baking. In 2020, Luna’s family faced uncertainty and unemployment, and she and her husband ended up returning to the kitchen to do what they loved.

As the Pandemic progressed, Luna began offering her baked goods to friends and family, which became the start of her incredible business. In less than three years, Luna’s LUNpias has grown in popularity throughout Southern California and neighboring states.

Luna has expressed her sincere gratitude for the support of her family and community, and stated, “Food, as a universal language, has allowed me to connect with diverse individuals, near and far, while doing what I cherish the most.”

As a representative of Supervisor Joel Anderson, the Supervisor of the County of San Diego, I drove to Poway to congratulate Luna and her family on their incredible work, and present her and her family with certificates of recognition at their ribbon cutting ceremony.

“It is my personal honor to present these certificates to Luna’s LUNpias to be commended on this day, October 28th, 2023. Congratulations, and on behalf of Supervisor Anderson, thank you,” I had said into the microphone, to the small crowd gathered outside their new Poway restaurant location.

Proud tears were shed by Luna, and the audience passionately cheered and applauded her. The restaurant itself had been packed with eager customers and filled with excited chattering, complete with a DJ playing music outside. Friends, families, and newcomers connected over Luna’s unique and innovative Filipino food.

Luna and her family business, born from the kindness of offering food to others during difficult times, has created a community of enthusiastic individuals with their incredible recipes and exceedingly warm-hearted and welcoming environment.
